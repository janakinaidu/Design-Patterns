Design Patterns:
===============
We have 4 types of Design Patterns in Java;
i)  Creational Design Pattern
ii) Strctural Design Pattern
iii)Behavioral Design Pattern
iv) J2EE Design Pattern

1.Creational Design Pattern:
--------------------------
-->Creational Design Pattern "deals with object creations".
-->By hiding object creation process from the Client Applications;
i)  Factory Method 
ii) Abstract Factory 
iii)Singleton  
iv) prototype 
v)  builder


2.Structural Design Patterns:
-----------------------------
i)  Adapter  
ii) Composite 
iii)decarator
iv) flyweight
v)  proxy

3.Behavioral
-------------
i)  Template Method
ii) Observer
iii)Strategy
iv) chain of Reposbility
v)  compound

4.j2ee:
-------

iii) Front controller

vii) Service Loader
